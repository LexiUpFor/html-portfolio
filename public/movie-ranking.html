My Top 4 Favorite Animated Movies

The Boxtrolls

A stop motion animated movie about trolls in boxes that are being exterminated
and up to Eggs and Winnie to save them.

Cars

A race car, Lightning Mcqueen, finds true friends in an odd town and learns its not all about winning.

Princess and The Frog

A girl who works hard to save money for her dream restaraunt gets accidently turned into a frog by a broke prince who promises her dream restaraunt but they both fall in love and work together to build the dream together.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

A story about The Pumpkin King, Jack Skellington, is looked up to by the town of Halloween. He finds himself in the town of Christmas and discovers a new holiday that he tries to integrate into his life. He then realizes Sally's prediction of a mistake he's making turns to be true and realizes she cares for him.
